How to Play

Balls appear in a special area based on your device size.

If it's red, right-click. If it's blue, left-click.

You must click within a time limit:

  • Easy: Very slow, hit at least 1
  • Medium: Faster, hit 50%
  • Hard: Fast, hit 70%
  • Impossible: 0.75s per ball, hit 100%
